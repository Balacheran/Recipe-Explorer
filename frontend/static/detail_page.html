Start by making the crispy onions by heating up the oil in a saucepan over a medium high heat and frying the sliced onions in batches until golden brown.Next, marinate your chicken by adding the chicken, 1/3 of the crispy onions, yoghurt, chilli powder, turmeric, garam masala, ginger garlic, lemon juice, one cinnamon stick, 8 cardamom pods, 1 green chilli sliced thin, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 4 cloves, chopped mint and coriander and a tsp of salt in a large pot.
